Patent Distribution in U.S.

This map shows the change in total number of patents among 50 states and one administrative district from 2012 to 2016. It is worth noting that California and New York states have the highest number of patents. We have identified 3 impact factors on number of patents: GDP per Capita, state agency research and development expenditure and graduate students from 25 to 34 years old with STEM backgrounds. The right-hand-side line charts display the fluctuations of patents numbers and three impact factors respectively over a period of 5 years.
