After exploring the spatial distribution of patents, why not look at what type of patent is taking up the majority of the total number of patents? Corporative Patent Classification (CPC) is a jointly managed classification scheme by European Patent Office and the United Sates Patent and Trademark Office. It has 9 main sections with subsections defining the nature of the patents.
